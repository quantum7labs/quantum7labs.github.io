🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Quantum7 Labs ("we", "our", "us") built the ABC Kids Learning and Color Bounce: Logic Dash 2D apps as free apps with optional in-app purchases. This page informs visitors about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

1. Information Collection

Our apps are designed with children's safety in mind. We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information from users, including children.

  • ❌ We do NOT collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • ❌ We do NOT collect location data
  • ❌ We do NOT collect photos, videos, or audio
  • ❌ We do NOT require user registration or login
  • ❌ We do NOT collect device identifiers for tracking purposes

2. Third-Party Services

Our apps use the following third-party services:

a) Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. For our children's app (ABC Kids Learning), ads are configured with child-directed treatment (COPPA compliant). AdMob may collect certain device information for ad serving purposes.

Google AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

b) Google Play In-App Purchases

Our apps offer in-app purchases processed through Google Play. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google. We do not store any payment information.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our ABC Kids Learning app is designed for children aged 2-6. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

  • ✅ No personal data collection from children
  • ✅ Ads are set to child-directed treatment
  • ✅ No behavioral advertising for children
  • ✅ No social media integration
  • ✅ No external links accessible to children

4. Data Storage

The only data stored locally on the device is:

  • App preferences (sound settings, theme preferences)
  • Premium purchase status (stored locally via SharedPreferences)
  • Quiz high scores (stored locally)

This data is stored only on the user's device and is not transmitted to any server.

5. Permissions

Our apps request only the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required for displaying ads and processing in-app purchases

We do NOT request access to camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive permissions.

6. Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting any information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
